The Safety Ambassadors conducted sessions for the students of middle school (grades 6-8) educating them about the UN Child Rights. More information about these sessions is listed in detail below. Along with these sessions, three of our mentors had the enthralling opportunity to host a webinar with the Dubai Police and the founder of the successful anti-cyberbullying app GoBubble - Henry Platten. Around 400 individuals across different age groups were present at this webinar. Our presentation on the theme ‘Children in the Digital World’ was met with a lot of positive feedback, and our mentors sincerely hope they were able to leave a lasting impact on the attendees.
