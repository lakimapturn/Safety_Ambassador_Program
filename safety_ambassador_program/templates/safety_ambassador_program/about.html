Our Cause

We, the Safety Ambassadors, aim to raise awareness and ensure the safety and security of children and to protect them from any violations they may be exposed to. We educate the students on the various laws of our land and make them aware of their rights and duties. Through the SAP program, we hope to empower students to not only stand up for their safety but also share any concerns experienced by their peers.

Our Logo

Our SAP Logo symbolizes our dedication to protecting our students online. The shield, which has been a symbol of protection from times immemorial, is seen to be embossed with microprocessor, highlighting our current topic of priority - protection of our students when they are using their devices. Technology has immense power to shape the world, but this very same power can be detrimental to us too, when not used correctly. Thus for this year, our safety ambassadors are channeling their efforts to equip the students with the skills to be safe online and utilize the many possibilities of the technologies which bring us all together.
