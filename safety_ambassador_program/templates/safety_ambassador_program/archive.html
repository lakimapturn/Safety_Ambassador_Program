SecureU Bracelet

Purpose: To alert the police authorities and the families of the braceletâ€™s wearer about their location even when in remote places through security, communication and innovation, the ideals of the Dubai Police.

Audience: Targeting specifically women, children, and those with special needs.

Description:

  1. A GPS integrated model, disguised as a bracelet since it is a commonly used accessory and can be hidden in plain sight.
  2. When a minimum amount of pressure is exerted on the sensor for 3 seconds then the GPS location will be sent to the 3 favourite contacts stored on the SecureU database.
  3. When the same amount of pressure is applied 2 times continuously then the location is sent to the central police authorities via the SecureU app.
  4. Can be adapted to multiple contexts by using the cuboid (as shown in the image) in other accessories like necklaces and armlets.
