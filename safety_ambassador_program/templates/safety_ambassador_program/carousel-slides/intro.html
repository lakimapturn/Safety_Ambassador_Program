Welcome to the Safety Ambassadors Program

The Safety Ambassadors Program (SAP) is an evidence-based program that harnesses the power of students delivering children rights, to prevent and stop violence and mistreatment. It is considered a social-emotional learning program and has been approved by many schools.

Initiated by the Dubai Police, in collaboration with the Knowledge and Human Development Authority (KHDA), SAP is a student-centered model that educates diverse social leaders with the skills to prevent and reduce violence delivering children rights . It is the nation's most effective student-led bullying & violence prevention program.

This program relies on the “inside-out” approach to improving school climate. Student bystanders see, hear, and know things adults don't, can intervene in ways adults can't and are often on the scene of an incident before an adult. They are a critical and necessary resource for positively impacting the crisis of bullying, violence in schools.
