Where Do We Come Across This Age Inappropriate Content?

1) Illegal Websites

Have you ever tried searching for a movie online? If you have, you'll know that it's usually difficult to find movies on platforms like YouTube. This is because the companies that make these movies don't want it circulating online. If you do come across a website that has lots of movies you can watch for free, it is most likely an illegal streaming service. In such websites, it is common to come across a number of pop up ads. 2) Gaming Apps/Websites

You may also come across inappropriate content while playing multiplayer games. When gaming server chats are open for anyone to join, it could expose you to people that behave in a way that makes you uncomfortable. This could be by using rude or explicit language, or any manner that makes you uncomfortable. Be cautious while using chats - strangers could be dishonest about things they tell you about themselves.

In these chats you could also find links, which are often spam messages generated by bots. These links could take you to dangerous, illegal websites. Be cautious and do not click on random links you come across and those that are not from a trusted source.
