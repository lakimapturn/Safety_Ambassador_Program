Digital Communication

Knowing how to communicate clearly and safely is very important, both online and offline.

Here are a few things you can do to take caution of your communicative skills online:

  1. Make sure you do not hurt anyone's feelings while talking to or of someone/ a subject
  2. When spreading news, please make sure you know what you are saying is true, and not false.
  3. Do not speak with any strangers online.
  4. Only communicate with people you have met (either in school or your neighbourhood)
