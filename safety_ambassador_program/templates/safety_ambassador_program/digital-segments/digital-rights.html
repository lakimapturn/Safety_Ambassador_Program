Digital Rights

We all have fundamental rights which are protected by law, they are necessary for all human beings to live a proper decent life. Some of these are right to life and right to education. Just like that, we also have digital rights, like a right to privacy in digital communities, a right to freely express our ideas and opinions, and a right to report anyone or anything that seems inappropriate. While our rights protect us, it is also important for us to respect everyone else’s rights and not to abuse our own.
