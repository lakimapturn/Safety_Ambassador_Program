Staying Safe Online

*Navigate this module along side a parent so that you and your parents can get a better idea of how your data is being used*

You can edit your personal data on your google account and control what other people can see. Google will also give you data and privacy suggestions so that you can stay safer on the web. This includes controlling which companies have your data and which passwords may need to be changed (may have been compromised or may have appeared in a data leak).

If you want to be extra safe, you can switch on the safe web feature as well, but it will be at the expense of sending more of your data to google instead. Talk to your parents before switching on the safe web feature.


It is also good to explore the privacy settings of all of your online accounts. You can start with Google accounts and YouTube. If you want to be extra safe when it comes to your data, then there is a website you can use - saymine.com Through this easy-to-use website you can get your data back from companies, as is your right.
