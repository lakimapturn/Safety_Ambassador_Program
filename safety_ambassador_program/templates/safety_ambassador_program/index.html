Digital Identity

Digital Identity is a set of characteristics- a digital profile of your own personality- that often helps the internet identify you as a unique individual.

Digital Safety

  1. Give no one your password, name, address, school name, or any other personal information about yourself or your family.
  2. On the internet, avoid chatting with strangers.
  3. Don't agree to meet anyone you've met online in person.
  4. If a profile asks for your name and address, don't fill it out.
  5. If you see something you believe your parents won't like, don't stay online.
  6. If you don't have your parents' consent, don't upload images of yourself online.
  7. Ask your parent or guardian if you have any questions regarding something you've read.
